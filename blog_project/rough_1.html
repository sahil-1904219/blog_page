STAIR CLIMBING ROBOT

When Robot climbs the stairs, the 3rd & 4th ultrasonic sensor are used to detect obstacles. Whether the obstacle detected is climbable or not, it will verify through the 1st ultrasonic sensor. If it the obstacle turns out to be climbable, like stairs, then it climbs and moves forward. If the obstacle is big or cannot be climbed upon, the 2nd ultrasonic sensor mounted on servo finds the other path and it proceeds.
